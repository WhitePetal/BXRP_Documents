Calculates the horizon of a circle orthogonally projected to a plane as seen from the origin on the plane.

Parameters
centerThe center of the circle projected onto the plane.
radiusThe radius of the circle.
UThe major axis of the ellipse formed by the projection of the circle.
VThe minor axis of the ellipse formed by the projection of the circle.
uv1The first horizon point expressed as factors of U and V .
uv2The second horizon point expressed as factors of U and V .
